🌍 Earthquake Emergency Training Simulator - 3D

📋 Scenario: ▼

While working in the office, a severe shaking (Earthquake) suddenly started!

What Will You Do?

1. 🏃‍♂️ Look for a safe area (e.g., under a desk) without panicking!

2. 🧱 Get under the desk and take the "Drop, Cover, Hold On" position.

  • DROP - Drop down onto your hands and knees.
  • COVER - Cover your head and neck.
  • HOLD ON - Hold on to a safe place (Desk leg, etc.).

3. ⏱️ Stay in this area until the shaking stops (about 15 seconds)!

ATTENTION: Running or standing during an earthquake is very risky!

📚 Lessons Learned:

  • Find a safe area (under a desk, etc.) without panicking during an earthquake.
  • Dropping down (DROP) prevents being thrown around.
  • Protecting your head and neck (COVER) protects from falling objects.
  • Holding onto a sturdy object (HOLD ON) keeps you safe during the shaking.
  • Heading outside or to stairs during an earthquake is risky.
